edvin molla

i'm edvin, and i'm currently studying applied mathematics at UCLA. i'm passionate about foundational machine learning models, and customizing them for applications in neuroscience, such as brain-machine interfaces, perceptual analysis, and memory.

complementing my passion for mathematics and foundational models is my desire to solve problems people have, by creating value through technology and novel applications. usually this takes the form of creating products rapidly and efficiently, and throwing it to the market for the ultimate validation.

this site is my public journal, where I jot down my thoughts and dilemmas. sometimes, i used to think my experiences and ideas were totally unique. but, as I read and talked to more people, I realized a lot of my dilemmas and mental struggles were universal experiences that are inherent to the human condition.
