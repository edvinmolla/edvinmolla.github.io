I'm Edvin, an undergraduate at UCLA studying applied mathematics with a focus in Machine Learning and its applications in multiple fields. I find great excitement in doing things that have a high probability of failure. I find it to be the quickest path to a unique learning experience.

This website serves as my public journal, covering a spectrum of topics from technical writings to life-related reflections. While I firmly believe that going through physical pain is the premiere way of exploring your character, I've found writing to be as potent, if not more, in my journey of personal discovery.
