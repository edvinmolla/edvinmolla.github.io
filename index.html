edvin molla

i'm Edvin, i'm originally from Albania, and i study applied mathematics at UCLA.

i'm passionate about the fundamentals of machine learning, and customizing them for applications in neuroscience, such as brain-machine interfaces, perceptual analysis, and memory.

i desire to solve problems people have, by creating value through technology and novel applications of it.

usually this takes the form of building products rapidly and efficiently, and throwing it to the market for the ultimate validation.

this site is my public journal, where i jot down my thoughts and dilemmas, usually in the form of analogies or logical analysis.

quite often, i used to think my experiences and ideas were totally unique. but, as I read and talked to more people, I realized a lot of my dilemmas and mental struggles were universal experiences that are inherent to the human condition.
