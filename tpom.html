Values are such an abstract concept. I sometimes think whether our ancestors, hundreds of thousands of years ago experienced such asbtract thoughts. Did they even need to? They must have been too busy surviving, protecting their children from bears, lions, and predators. They didn't have much free time on their hands to sit down and think about their values, much less do what I'm doing right now.

~

However, values, morals, principles, and such entities are fascinating to thing about. How do they come about exactly? How do we learn and import values from our parents and society? How do we comform to society through exposure? What does this process look like?
